Help

After reading this page, you will be able to fully use the Seizure Predictor online account.
The software is extremely user friendly. You just need to login to see your prediction results. To login please go the Home page , enter your patient ID and click login.

A pop-up window will appear asking for a User Name and Password. Please provide this information (note, this is different than your patient ID) and click Log In.

Once you are logged into your account, the front page will indicate the Seizure Predictor result from the latest EEG reading based on the background color and the written result on the bottom of the page. A status of "Ok" and green background color indicates normal brain activity. A status of "Warning" and yellow background color indicates slightly abnormal brain activity and a seizure may occur. A status of "Seizure!" and red background color indicates brain activty has been measured for a seizure to occur.

There are two tabs in the upper right-hand corner. From here, you can click on "EEG Graphs". This will open up a new tab in the browser displaying the graphical representation of the latest analyzed data. You can also click "Log Out" to log out of your Seizure Predictor account and return back to the main Home Page.

If you have any questions please visit the frequent ask questions site or Contact Us
