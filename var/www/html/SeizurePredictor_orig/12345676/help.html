Help Page

Seizure Predictor is here to be your helpful, guiding hand. As someone experiencing seizures, it is important to have hte ability to monitor your well-being, to ensure that you live a risk-free and healthy life. As it is known that seizures can occur unexpectedly, Seizure Predictor is here to help signify if brain activity measured by your EEG device is at levels indicating a seizure is about to occur. When having the Seizure Predictor by your side, you will have the proper amount of time to stop what you are doing to achieve a safe position before anything dangerous may occur.

Although the Seizure Predictor attempts to be as accurate as possible while analyzing the data, the given result is at most one minute old. This means that your personal EEG recorder sends data to the Seizure Predictor service every 60 seconds. This is expected to improve in the next release of the service.


Frequently Asked Questions

What does each color shown in the Predictor Status page indicate?

  Green: Brain activity is analyzed as expected. There is no abrupt activity and one can expect to experience a seizure in the following hours.
  Yellow: Brain activity is measured to be more sporadic and changing than is thought to be normal. Although brain activity may decrease, be aware that a seizure may be experienced if brain activity is not calmed down.
  Red: Immediately obtain a safe position with minimal potential to hurt oneself if seizure occurs. Indicated by brain activity in this state, a seizure may be experienced at any moment.
